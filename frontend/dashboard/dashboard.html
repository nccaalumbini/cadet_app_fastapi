Introduction
The Youth Leadership Program was designed to develop leadership skills among NCCAA cadets and selected students from local schools. The program focused on teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills through various activities and workshops.
Objectives
  • To develop leadership qualities among youth
  • To enhance teamwork and collaboration skills
  • To improve communication and public speaking abilities
  • To foster a sense of civic responsibility
Execution
The program was conducted over four days with a series of workshops, team-building exercises, and practical activities. Experts from various fields were invited to conduct sessions on leadership, communication, and problem-solving. The participants were divided into groups to work on projects that addressed community issues.
Achievements
The program was highly successful with participants showing significant improvement in their leadership capabilities. Several community projects initiated during the program are ongoing. Feedback from participants and guests was overwhelmingly positive, with requests to make this an annual event.
